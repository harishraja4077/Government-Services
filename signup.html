Before You Register

Ensure you have your official papers handy to verify identity details and claim direct benefits.

Required Verifiable Documents

  • National Smart ID Number / Social Security ID
  • Active cellular phone number (to receive secure SMS OTP codes)
  • Digital scan of Address Proof (e.g. Utility bill issued within 90 days)

Account Approval Timeline

The system audits your registration parameters automatically.

1. Register Account Details

Fill out the signup form and submit your national identity number parameters.

2. Confirm OTP Email Code

Confirm the 6-digit confirmation pin sent to your email inbox immediately.

3. National Registry Audit

The database matches your smart ID with census records to confirm authenticity.

4. Active Citizen Profile

Your portal dashboard opens immediately to apply for and track active services.
