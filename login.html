Cybersecurity Architecture

This portal is engineered under rigorous national cybersecurity guidelines to protect citizen data privacy.

256-bit Encryption

All database records and network data streams utilize modern TLS 1.3 certificates to prevent eavesdropping.

Auto-Timeout Periods

Accounts automatically lock after 15 minutes of idle session status to mitigate unauthorized workspace access.

Multi-Factor Checks

Confirm payments or critical document downloads using standard SMS / Email verification codes.

Protect your credentials. Portal administration and department executives will never request your passwords, debit card pins, or national biometric credentials via text, telephone, or email.
