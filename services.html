The Application Flow

Four simple operational phases to receive your official credentials online.

1. Register Account

Sign up securely on the citizen portal using your National Identity number and verify credentials.

2. Choose & Complete Form

Select the desired service, fill in the digital application form, and upload scanned support documents.

3. Pay & Verify

Complete any administrative filing fees online via secure banking. Portal admins will review details.

4. Final Issue

Receive your certified digital document in your locker, or trace its dispatch details via secure mail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear information regarding portal access, fee parameters, and application timelines.

How long does the average portal verification take?

Most basic filings, such as municipal tax submissions and birth registry applications, are verified automatically within 24 hours. Complex documents like passport checks or land title audits require manual agency verification and generally process within 7 to 10 working days.

Are transaction fees paid on this portal secure?

Yes. All banking actions on our site utilize 256-bit SSL encryption standards. Payments are routed through secure, PCI-DSS compliant federal banking gateways. Your card or credential details are never logged on our database.

What is the digital locker and how does it function?

The Digital Locker is a personal storage folder attached directly to your Citizen account. Once an application is approved, the official administrative certificates (e.g., driver license, tax slip) are digitally signed and loaded directly into your locker. You can print, view, or share these files at any time.
