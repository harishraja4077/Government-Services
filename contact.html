Grievance Lifecycle

Tracking the lifecycle of your filed grievance tickets from initial receipt to final solution.

1. Submission & Auto-Log

Ticket is verified, logged into the system, and a tracking ID code is auto-emailed to the citizen.

2. Officer Assignment

The ticket is routed to the corresponding department's grievance officer for factual checks.

3. Inspection & Remediation

The assigned officer inspects the core complaint and prepares a correction plan.

4. SLA Close

The ticket is closed. The citizen receives an official resolution statement and can request reviews.
